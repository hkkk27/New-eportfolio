Sleep Calculator
Project Overview

This Sleep Time Calculator is a web-based tool that helps users determine the best times to go to bed in order to wake up feeling refreshed. The tool calculates sleep times based on a user's desired wake-up time, considering the sleep cycle duration of 90 minutes. It provides multiple sleep time options, ranging from 1 to 6 complete sleep cycles, to ensure users can align their sleep schedule with optimal rest periods.

Features:
1. User Input: Users enter their wake-up time in a 24-hour format.
2. Sleep Cycle Calculation: The tool calculates sleep times based on 90-minute cycles.
3. Multiple Sleep Options: It provides suggestions for when to fall asleep to complete a full sleep cycle before waking up.
4. Output Display: The results are shown in a list format for easy understanding, offering users different sleep time options.

The design is user-friendly, with a modern, clean interface featuring dark-themed colors and easy-to-read typography. The tool is meant to assist users in improving their sleep habits by calculating the best sleep times to optimize their rest.

Research Essay
Project Overview

Research on Data Collection and Its Impact on Freedom of Thought

I conducted research exploring how data collection in today's digital age impacts individual autonomy and reinforces societal biases. The study examined how personal data is used by corporations to predict and influence consumer behavior, as well as its effects on political decision-making. By analyzing the role of algorithms, AI, and neuromarketing, my research highlighted the growing risks posed by unchecked data collection, including the reinforcement of existing inequalities and the creation of echo chambers. This project aims to raise awareness about the ethical concerns surrounding data use and the importance of regulating it.
